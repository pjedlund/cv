Services

Snow Interactive is a new media firm run by Johan Edlund. He started the firm in 1999 while living in New York City, but has since returned to his native country Sweden and the town of Malmö.

Johan enjoys creating simple, elegant web sites and web applications. He takes pride in providing solutions that offers both pleasing visuals as well as clean, valid code. He believes that design and usability are two sides of the same coin.
